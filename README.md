🎓 Student Social Media & Mental Health Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes student social media usage, mental health, stress levels, study habits, lifestyle patterns, and country-wise behavior. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and actionable insights to help educational institutions, counselors, and researchers understand factors affecting student well-being.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze social media platform usage and engagement.
Evaluate student mental health and stress levels.
Study academic behavior and study habits.
Assess lifestyle factors such as sleep and physical activity.
Compare student behavior across different countries.
Generate data-driven insights and recommendations.
📊 Dashboards Included
1. Executive Overview Dashboard

Provides a high-level summary of student behavior, mental health, social media usage, and key KPIs.

2. Social Media Analytics Dashboard

Analyzes platform popularity, usage hours, daily unlocks, and purpose of social media usage.

3. Mental Health Analytics Dashboard

Evaluates mental health scores, stress distribution, gender analysis, and academic-level comparisons.

4. Lifestyle Analytics Dashboard

Examines sleep patterns, study habits, and physical activity levels.

5. Country Analytics Dashboard

Compares student well-being, stress levels, and mental health performance across countries.

6. Drill Through Analysis

Provides detailed student-level analysis for deeper exploration of selected countries.

📈 Key Insights
Students spend an average of 5.08 hours daily on social media.
Instagram is the most popular platform among students.
A significant number of students fall into High and Very High Stress categories.
The average Mental Health Score is 6.23.
Sleep patterns and study habits influence student well-being.
Student behavior and mental health vary across different countries.
